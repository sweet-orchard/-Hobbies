LOVING CATS

Another passion of mine is collecting pictures of different cats that I meet near my home. I have lots of pictures from my younger years to now. I don’t have my own cat, so I just enjoy petting and holding other people's cats. It looks so cute, doesn’t it? Each cat has its own unique personality, and I love capturing their expressions and quirks in photos. It’s also a great way to connect with my neighbors and share our love for these adorable animals. Collecting these pictures has become a cherished hobby that brings me joy and relaxation.

ART DRAWING

Why do I like painting?

My father is an artist, so I think I have had a little bit of talent for drawing since birth. I like to paint because I can express my emotions on paper. When I’m sad, I want to draw. Whenever I’m happy, I paint. I just forget about everything else. Drawing makes me feel in control. It is the only thing that helps me enjoy a bit of freedom. It makes me feel like I can make anything happen and design my own world the way I like it. In short, it’s just relaxing and calming.
