READING BOOKS

Why do I like reading?​

I’m not really a fan of reading. I mean, I read, but not often. I like books from which I can take something and use in my own life, like those about organization, time management, how to achieve something, or what to say in a particular situation. Reading helps me improve my memory, grow my vocabulary, and just have fun when I’m bored.
