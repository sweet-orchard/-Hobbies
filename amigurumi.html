AMIGURUMI KNITTING

It’s a popular type of crochet. The word ”Amigurumi” is a combinations of two Japanese words: ami, which means crocheted or knitted; and nuigurumi, a staffed doll. Usually, it takes form of an animal or a cute fantastical toy.
